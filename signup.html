About Subscription ID

What is a Subscription ID?

A Subscription ID is a unique identification number assigned to you by our platform after you place a request. You can locate your Subscription ID at the bottom of your order receipt.

How does it work?

The Subscription ID allows us to locate your order and verify your identity efficiently.

How to Get Your Subscription ID

To obtain your Subscription ID, submit a request to Captrax by providing your requirements on the pricing page. Follow the steps outlined there to complete your request. For detailed instructions visit this article.
